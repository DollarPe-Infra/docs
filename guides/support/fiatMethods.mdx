  # Fiat Payment Methods
</Highlight>

## Understanding Fiat Payment Options

DollarPe supports multiple fiat payment methods to provide flexibility and convenience for your transactions. This guide explains the available payment options, their characteristics, and best practices for integration.

<Note>
  All transactions are processed in **INR** (Indian Rupees) and are available 24x7, except for NEFT which follows RBI banking hours.
  
  For any payment-related queries, please contact our support team at support@dollarpe.com
</Note>

## Supported Payment Methods

We support the following payment methods for **INDIA**:
