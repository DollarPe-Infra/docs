<Highlight># Stablecoins and Blockchains</Highlight>

DollarPe supports multiple stablecoins across various blockchain networks to provide you with flexibility and optimal transaction costs. This guide explains which combinations are supported and best practices for using them in your integration.

<Warning>
  **CRITICAL SECURITY NOTICE**: 
  - Deposits sent to unsupported asset/blockchain pairs will be permanently lost 
  - Always verify the blockchain network before sending any funds 
  - Double-check wallet addresses to ensure they match the expected asset/network pair 
  - Funds sent to incorrect addresses or networks CANNOT be recovered
</Warning>

## Supported Stablecoins and Blockchains

We support the following **stablecoins** on these blockchains:

| Asset    | eth | polygon | arbitrum | tron | solana | bsc | sepolia [Sandbox ONLY] |
| :------- | :-: | :-----: | :------: | :--: | :----: | :-: | :-----------------------: |
| **USDT** | ✅  |   ✅    |    ✅    |  ✅  |   ✅   | ✅  |            ✅             |

<Note>
  The Sepolia network is only available in our **Sandbox environment** for testing
  purposes. Do not use it for production transactions.
</Note>
