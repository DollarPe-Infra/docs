<Highlight>
  # Fiat Methods
</Highlight>

<Note>
  All transactions are processed in **INR** (Indian Rupees) and are available 24x7, except for NEFT which follows RBI banking hours.
  
  For any payment-related queries, please contact our support team at support@dollarpe.com
</Note>

We support the following payment methods for **INDIA**:

| Payment Method | Description | Processing Time | Limits |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | -------------- | --------------------------------- |
| **UPI** | Unified Payments Interface | Instant | Min: ₹100<br/>Max: ₹1,00,000 |
| **IMPS** | Immediate Payment Service | Instant | Min: ₹100<br/>Max: ₹5,00,000 |
| **NEFT** | National Electronic Funds Transfer | 30 mins | Min: ₹100<br/>No Upper Limit |
| **RTGS** | Real Time Gross Settlement | Real-time | Min: ₹2,00,000<br/>No Upper Limit |
