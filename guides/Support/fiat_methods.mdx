<Highlight>
  # Fiat Methods
</Highlight>

<Note>
  All transactions are processed in **INR** (Indian Rupees) and are available 24x7, except for NEFT which follows RBI banking hours.
  
  For any payment-related queries, please contact our support team at support@dollarpe.com
</Note>

We support the following payment methods for **INDIA**:

<Accordion title="1. UPI (Unified Payments Interface)">
  - Processing Time: Instant
  - Minimum Amount: ₹100
  - Maximum Amount: ₹1,00,000
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="2. IMPS (Immediate Payment Service)">
  - Processing Time: Instant
  - Minimum Amount: ₹100
  - Maximum Amount: ₹5,00,000
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="3. NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer)">
  - Processing Time: 30 mins
  - Minimum Amount: ₹100
  - No Upper Limit
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="4. RTGS (Real Time Gross Settlement)">
  - Processing Time: Real-time
  - Minimum Amount: ₹2,00,000
  - No Upper Limit
</Accordion>
